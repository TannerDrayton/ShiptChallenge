TestCase1 -- Login page
Descroption: A registered user be able to successfuly log into their shipt account.

Pre-Condition: User must be a registered member with a valid username and password.
Assumption: User is using a supported browser.

Test steps: 1) Navigate to shipt.com.
            2) Click on the "LOG IN" button at the top right of the the screen.
            3) In the "EMAIL" field, enter the email of the registered user.
            4) In the "PASSWORD" field, enter the password of the registered user.
            5) Click on the "LOG IN" button.

Expected result: URL displays "shop.shipt.com" and user's home page is displayed.
